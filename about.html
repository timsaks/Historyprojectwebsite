About Us

Our History Project

We created this website to study how American life changed from the 1940s through the 1970s. Our goal is to connect major events to the people, choices, and ideas that shaped modern America.

Our Focus

Explaining Change Over Time

Each decade page highlights two exhibits that show a different kind of change, from wartime work and civil rights to the Space Race, Watergate, and the energy crisis.

Our Approach

Using Visual Evidence

We chose images that help make the history easier to understand. The descriptions explain why each exhibit matters and how it connects to the essential question.
