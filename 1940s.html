Decade Exhibit

The 1940s

World War II reshaped American life at home and abroad, while the decade's ending placed the United States in a new position of global power.

1942

Women and Wartime Work

With millions of men serving overseas, women entered factories, shipyards, and offices in large numbers. Posters like this encouraged wartime labor and helped create a lasting debate about gender roles after the war ended.

1945

The Atomic Age Begins

The atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki brought World War II to a close in the Pacific and introduced a new era of nuclear power. The event changed diplomacy, military strategy, and public fears for decades.
