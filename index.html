1940s to 1970s

How have women’s roles in the workforce expanded due to social and policy changes from the 1940s to the 1970s?

The role of women has changed drastically throughout the course of US history, specifically in the jobs and careers women have been able to hold. From Rosies during WWII to Betty Friedan's Feminine Mystique, women's place in society was constantly changing and evolving throughout the 40s to the 70s as they pushed for more opportunity and social change. Read through the exhibits to get a closer look on how women in the workforce have evolved.
